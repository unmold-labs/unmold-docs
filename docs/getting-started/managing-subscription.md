# Manage Subscription

Subscription plans control usage limits in Unmold, such as how many modules you can publish.

## Check Your Current Plan

To view your subscription:

1. Open the Unmold console
2. Go to **Settings → Subscription**

## When You Need to Upgrade

You may need to upgrade if:

* You reach the module limit on the Free plan

## Upgrade Your Plan

To upgrade:

1. Go to **Settings → Subscription**
2. Click **Change Subscription**
3. Select a plan and complete checkout with Stripe

See the [subscription](../subscriptions) page for more plan details.

## Manage Billing

If you are on a paid plan:

* Use **Manage Payment** in the Subscription section
* Update payment methods or view billing details

## Downgrade

You can switch back to the Free plan at any time from the same **Change Subscription** dialog.

## Notes

* Limits are enforced when publishing new modules
* Existing published modules are not affected by plan changes
