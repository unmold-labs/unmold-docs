Today we’re opening up [**Unmold.dev**](https://unmold.dev) — a CLI-first module registry for OpenTofu and Terraform — now in **public beta**.

<!-- truncate -->

Unmold is an early attempt at a simpler, more flexible way to publish and manage modules. Instead of relying on UI-driven workflows and hidden processes, it’s designed to work directly from your CLI and CI pipelines, so it can fit naturally into how you already build and ship infrastructure.

### What’s available today

* **Private registry**
  Compatible with Terraform and OpenTofu module specifications

* **CLI for publishing and versioning**
  Lightweight and scriptable

* **GitHub Action**
  Basic CI integration for automated publishing

### Why we’re building this

We’ve found that existing solutions can feel restrictive when trying to automate or customize workflows. Unmold is an exploration of a more open, low-level approach — giving you building blocks rather than prescribing a system.

### Pricing

* **Free**: one private module, unlimited versions
* **Pro**: higher limits and additional lifecycle features

For more details, see the [Subscriptions](../docs/subscriptions.md) page.
